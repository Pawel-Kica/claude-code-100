Use Charlie Munger inversion thinking process to tackle a problem or help user to make a decision.

It's the process of solving complex problems by looking at them in reverse. Instead of asking how to succeed, you ask how to fail or how to cause the worst outcome, and then rigorously avoid those failure points

Run a `/grilling` session, one inverted question at a time.
Stop when the forward move is obvious, then state it in one line.

If invoked indirectly, skip the grilling and just tackle the problem with this mindset.
