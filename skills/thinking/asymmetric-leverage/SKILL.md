Find the asymmetric leverage. 80/20 rule: a small share of inputs drives most of the output.

Two moves, both required:
- Name the vital 20%: which few inputs actually move the result?
- Name the 80% to cut: what you'll deliberately stop doing, so the leverage shows up.

Run a `/grilling` session, one question at a time, until the vital few and the cut are clear. Then state both in one line.

If invoked indirectly, skip the grilling and just tackle the problem with this mindset.
